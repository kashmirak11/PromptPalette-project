About Prompt Palette

Our mission is to democratize creativity through artificial intelligence.

Our Story

AI Palette was founded in 2025 with a simple yet ambitious goal: to make advanced AI image generation accessible to everyone, from professional designers to casual creators.

What started as a research project quickly evolved into a powerful platform that has helped thousands of users bring their creative visions to life.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI-generated imagery, constantly improving our models and expanding our feature set to meet the evolving needs of our users.

Our Values

Innovation

We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible with AI technology.

Accessibility

We believe powerful AI tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Transparency

We are committed to ethical AI development and being transparent about our technology.
